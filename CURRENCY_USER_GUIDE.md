## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Will my currency preference be saved?

**A:** Yes! Your currency choice is saved in your browser. When you return to the website, it will automatically use your preferred currency.

### Q: Can I switch currency after adding items to cart?

**A:** Absolutely! You can switch currency at any time. Your cart items remain the same, only the prices update to reflect the new currency.

### Q: Is the exchange rate accurate?

**A:** The exchange rate (1 USD = 83 INR) is an approximate rate. For the exact amount charged to your card, check the payment page during checkout.

### Q: Do I need to create an account to use the currency switcher?

**A:** No! The currency switcher works for all visitors, whether you're logged in or not.

### Q: Can I pay in INR?

**A:** Currently, all payments are processed in USD through Stripe. However, you can browse and shop in INR, and the conversion to USD happens automatically at checkout.

### Q: What if I clear my browser data?

**A:** If you clear your browser's localStorage, your currency preference will reset to USD (the default). Simply switch back to INR if needed.

### Q: Does the currency switcher work on mobile?

**A:** Yes! The currency switcher is fully functional on mobile devices. Just open the mobile menu to access it.

### Q: Can I see prices in both currencies at once?

**A:** Currently, you can only view prices in one currency at a time. Switch between USD and INR to compare prices.

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: I don't see the currency switcher

**Solution:**
- **Desktop**: Look in the top-right corner of the header
- **Mobile**: Open the menu (‚ò∞ icon) and check the top
- **Try refreshing** the page if it doesn't appear

### Problem: Prices aren't changing when I click the button

**Solution:**
- **Check the button text** - it should toggle between USD and INR
- **Refresh the page** and try again
- **Clear your browser cache** if the issue persists

### Problem: My currency preference isn't saving

**Solution:**
- **Enable cookies/localStorage** in your browser settings
- **Don't use incognito/private mode** - preferences won't save
- **Check browser privacy settings** - some settings block localStorage

### Problem: Prices look wrong after switching

**Solution:**
- **Verify the exchange rate**: 1 USD = 83 INR
- **Check the currency symbol**: $ for USD, ‚Çπ for INR
- **Refresh the page** if prices seem incorrect
